Stephanie Kirsche is a self-published author who calls Cicero, New York home. A native of Elmira, she and her husband made their way to the Syracuse area in 2020 after spending five transformative years in San Francisco, where she honed her leadership skills managing coffee shops and working alongside diverse office teams.

When she's not writing, Stephanie can be found testing her knowledge at local pub trivia nights, cheering on the Yankees, or discovering hidden gems among the pristine lakes of the Finger Lakes region. Her varied experiences—from small-town roots to big-city adventures—bring a unique perspective to her storytelling.

Recent Work

Sky is Falling

Skylar Nash, a bestselling romance novelist with over 80,000 followers on social media, lives in a stylish Soho loft filled with trendy items. After a seven-year relationship, she’s struggling to move on and find inspiration for her next book. On her publisher's advice, she travels to Paris to seek both creative and emotional renewal, hoping the city's charm will help her craft a new love story.

Pictures of You

28 years old, Rachel Kelly is trying to rebuild her life after an accident took away her memories from the past eleven years. With only her childhood memories, dreams, and photos to guide her, Rachel works to reconnect with her past. However, she quickly realizes that things are not as she remembers them.

Deadly Sins

A Witch on the Run encounters more than she bargained for when she stops for a bite to eat at a location establishment with a down-on-his-luck owner. With a means of money and a few new allies, Ariadne Jones starts to think Dern Hill might be a good spot for her to lay low. That is, until dead bodies start popping up all over town. With the help of a blue-eyed Deputy Sheriff and a ragtag new coven, Ariadne must use her unusual talents to save those she has come to care for before it's too late.
